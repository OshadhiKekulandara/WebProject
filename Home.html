OUR
HISTORY

The VOGUE journey began in 1954 as a
lingerie boutique in Alingsås, Sweden. From
there, we have added womenswear, kids and
men’s clothing, cosmetics and accessories to
our crisp, chic, contemporary collections. That
one lingerie boutique is now joined by nearly
500 stores offering the complete Fashion
collection around the world.

The VOGUE production process is a global

WHAT WE DO



We do not any factories and work with independent suppliers and factories
around the world. Our on-site fashion staff works alongside local factoryemployees
to ensure our designs are achieved under the best operating methods. On thhe factory
floor, we begin by knitting the fabrics weuse. The knitted frabic is formed as the
finished garment begins to take shape. The craftmanship involved inproducing the
maximum number of garments out of each piece of fabric is a true art an one that
we value grately.
